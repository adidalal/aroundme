What's this all about?



AroundMe is all about living in the present. Want to eat now? We're there. Nightlife, Arts, Shopping, or Sightseeing? We got you covered. Tap into Foursquare's massive location database from anywhere, with nothing more than a simple text.

How do I use it?



  • Think: of something you want to find (ex. park, bar, food)
  • Text: (646) 681 - 2773 with whatever you are interested in... ex. "Thai food near Philadelphia, PA" or "Pizza in NYC"
  • Receive: A response within seconds! This will provide you with a name and address, and of course a link for more information.
